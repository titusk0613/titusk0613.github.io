Summary

"On the Come Up" is an incisive and engrossing perspective of the life of a black teenage girl.
Sixteen-year-old Bri wants to be one of the greatest rappers of all time
As a daughter of an underground hip-hop legend who died right before he hit big, Bri has a lot to live up to.
But, it's difficult for her. Being labeled a hoodlum, having little to no food, and her mom losing her job.
So Bri pours her anger and frustration into her first song. Unfortunately for her it ends up goig viral for
all the wrong reasons. Bri soon finds her self in the middle of a controversy. The media portrays her as
the villain. Things get worse as they get an eviction notice. Now, not only does she need to make it, she has to.
